All Services

Our five-star luxury hotel offers an unparalleled experience, where every service is designed to cater to your every need with exceptional attention to detail. From world-class dining and rejuvenating spa treatments to personalized concierge assistance and luxurious accommodations, we provide a wide range of amenities to ensure your stay is both indulgent and effortless. Whether you're relaxing by the pool, enjoying exclusive access to our executive lounges, or experiencing the finest in-room entertainment, we strive to make every moment of your stay memorable and extraordinary. Below are the list of services offered:

Childcare Services

Ensure a fun and safe experience for your children with our professional babysitting and kids’ club programs. Supervised activities, games, and creative workshops keep young guests entertained while parents enjoy some well-deserved relaxation.

Concierge Services

Enhance your stay by our concierge team's assistance with everything from booking local tours and securing reservations to providing expert recommendations and arranging transportation. Let us make your trip effortless and unforgettable.

Dining Options

Explore a culinary journey with our diverse dining venues, including gourmet restaurants, casual cafés, a rooftop bar with stunning views, and an extensive buffet. Our menus feature local specialties and international favorites to satisfy every craving.

Event Hosting

Make your events extraordinary with our elegant banquet halls and state-of-the-art meeting rooms. Whether it’s a dream wedding, a business conference, or a private celebration, our experienced event planners will ensure every detail is perfect.

Exclusive Club Lounges

Escape to our VIP club lounges, offering a serene space to unwind or focus on work. Enjoy complimentary snacks, premium beverages, and personalized service in a luxurious setting.

Gift Shop and Boutique

Shop at our on-site boutique for unique souvenirs, luxury items, and travel essentials. Whether you need a last-minute gift or a special keepsake, you’ll find something memorable.

Housekeeping

Relax in a spotless and inviting room with our daily cleaning, laundry services, and thoughtful turndown service. We take pride in maintaining the highest standards of cleanliness and comfort.

In-Room Entertainment

Elevate your downtime with access to premium TV channels, streaming platforms, and gaming consoles. Our in-room entertainment options are designed for your relaxation and enjoyment.

Personalized Welcome Amenities

Start your stay with a warm and thoughtful welcome, including complimentary gifts like fruit baskets, champagne, or handwritten notes. Let us add a personal touch to your special occasions.

Recreational Activities

Discover endless opportunities for fun and relaxation, from refreshing swims in the pool to guided tours and exciting sports. Guests of all ages will find something to enjoy during their stay.

Room Service

Savor the convenience of freshly prepared meals, snacks, and beverages delivered directly to your room. Whether it’s breakfast in bed or a late-night snack, we’ve got you covered.

Sports Court Access

Stay active with access to our state-of-the-art sports courts, perfect for recreational games or professional practice. Enjoy a variety of activities in a modern, well-equipped setting.

Valet Parking

Enjoy stress-free parking with our professional valet team, who handle your vehicle with care. Relax knowing that your car is safe and readily accessible whenever you need it.

Wellness and Spa Services

Rejuvenate your mind and body with luxurious spa treatments, from relaxing massages and facials to beauty therapies. Access our fitness centers and yoga classes to complete your wellness experience.

Our hotel offers a luxurious and inviting retreat, blending sophisticated design with exceptional service. With a focus on comfort and style, we provide a range of top-tier amenities to ensure a memorable experience for every guest. Whether you're here for business or leisure, our attentive staff and world-class facilities will make your stay truly extraordinary.
